Entering non-OSCAR submissions
Trick of the trade:
Who else can check for paper submissions?
Who else can enter them into OSCAR?
ADMIN MEMBERS!
An excellent job for Admin Members is to have them periodically check the paper submission box, and transpose those submissions into OSCAR.  Furthermore, they can print out the confirmation page and put the printout in the member’s internal mail slot.
